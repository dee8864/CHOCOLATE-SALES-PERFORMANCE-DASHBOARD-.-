# CHOCOLATE-SALES-PERFORMANCE-DASHBOARD-.-

🍫 Excited to share my latest Data Analytics Project — CHOCOLATE SALES PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD built in Microsoft Power BI.

This end-to-end business intelligence dashboard transforms raw transactional data of a global chocolate company into a fully interactive, multi-page analytical report — covering everything from revenue and profitability to operations and order health across 6 countries and 25 salespeople.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

📌 PURPOSE OF THE PROJECT

The goal was to answer a real business question:
"Where is revenue coming from, who is driving it, which products are truly profitable, and where are the operational risks hiding?"

🔑 KEY CONCRETE FINDINGS FROM THIS DASHBOARD
📍 Total Revenue: ₹44.69M | Gross Profit: ₹20.24M | Margin: 45.29%
📍 Top Product: Peanut Butter Cubes — ₹3.75M revenue
📍 Top Markets: UK (23.59%) & India (23.26%) drive nearly half of global revenue
📍 Team Gap: Delish (₹13.5M) earns 2× more than Tempo (₹6.4M)
📍 Sales Gap: Barr Faughny (₹3.66M) vs Van Tuxwell (₹0.56M) — 6.5× difference
📍 Risk Signal: 371 cancelled orders = 4.69% cancellation rate — untracked before this dashboard
📍 Hidden Insight: Bites category earns near-equal profit to Bars on 3× fewer boxes — highest margin per box

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 KEY TAKEAWAYS — WHAT I LEARNED

→ Data modelling is the foundation. A clean star schema made all DAX measures accurate and reusable across every page.
→ DAX is more than SUM. CALCULATE, DIVIDE, DATEADD, SUMX, and RELATED were all essential for real business metrics.
→ Design matters as much as data. A themed background, consistent color palette, and clear page hierarchy make the difference between a report people read and one they trust.
→ The most important insight in this dataset — profit margin — was completely invisible until DAX was used to calculate it from the cost column buried in the dimension table.
→ Order_Status data was entirely unused in the original file. Adding one donut chart revealed a 4.69% cancellation problem that no one was watching.

✅ CONCLUSION — WHAT THIS DASHBOARD IS REALLY SHOWING
The business looks healthy on the surface — but the data reveals exactly where to act: invest more in UK & India, review Tempo team performance, investigate cancellations, and prioritise high-margin Bites products. That is what analytics does — it does not just describe what happened, it tells you where to look next.
